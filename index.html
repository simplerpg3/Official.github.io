What is Cloud RPG?

Cloud RPG is a community which is currently creating content, RendHeaven, a veteran scratcher from 2010, created this community with the game "Simple RPG" and a ton of people loved it, so he created the second and third part which gain a lot of popularity.

Who is RendHeaven?

RendHeaven is a scratch veteran who enjoys creating RPGS and he owns 3 famous games that were featured by scratch, these games are Simple RPG, Simple RPG 2 and Simple RPG3, he is currently working on a game with a pokemon engine to keep his community alive

How can I join the community?

Well, we have a community tab in the website, click it and it will redirect you to the community page, click on the studio button, there you can find games created by rendheaven and other fangames, tips and more usefull stuff, there is a chat where you can ask people to co-op with you in Simple RPG 3 or pvp in Simple RPG 2, make sure to follow the studio!

How can I be a manager?

To be a manager you need to be really active in the studio.
